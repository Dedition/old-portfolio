Intro

Life is a series of opportunities, choices and decisions. It's for this reason I always intend to give everything I do my all as you only ever have one chance to make the most of your day. Software Engineering gave me the opportunity to turn my life motto into something more tangible and focused. It's given me leverage to contribute on a global scale and make an impact in ways previously unimaginable. My core competencies are in Redux, React, Node, Express, SQL, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy, Python, Javascript & HTML/CSS. By the way, check out my awesome work.

Throughout my time as an engineer, I've created several full-stack projects and have been able to maintain their scalability and performance in multiple work environments. If you're here, then I'm looking forward to seeing how I can work with you.

Work

Who-Dis is an application that emulates Discord, a message-sharing app. It's a full-stack application that allows users to create accounts, send messages, and view messages sent by other users. The app is built with React, Redux, Node, Python, SQLAlchemy, JavaScript, React-Router and more. It's a great way to learn about the MVC architecture and how to build a full-stack application despite being a Flux React architecture overall. Facebook Cap is an application that emulates Facebook.
